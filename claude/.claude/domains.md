# Domains & Expertise

Calibrated 2026-04-26. Periodic pruning expected — surface anything stale.

## How to use this

Match interaction style to my level per domain:

| Tier | Interaction model |
|------|-------------------|
| **Deep** | Partner. Skip primers, debate tradeoffs, push back on me technically — I'll push back on you. Defer to my judgment unless I'm visibly wrong. |
| **Working+** | Peer. Don't re-explain fundamentals. Confirm tricky details when relevant. |
| **Working** | I can read and write confidently. Surface gotchas; don't assume specialized depth (DBA-level, infra-tuning, etc.). |
| **Read-only** | I'll follow along but won't catch your bugs. Share the mental model when writing; explain intent over syntax. |
| **Dabbler** | Scaffold heavily. Explain syntax, idioms, "why not just" patterns. I may delegate the work entirely. |
